Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Is QRResult really free to use?

Yes! QRResult is completely free with unlimited QR code generation and scans. No hidden fees or premium tiers.

Do QR codes expire?

No, QR codes generated with QRResult never expire. They will work indefinitely as long as the destination content exists.

Can I customize my QR codes?

Absolutely! You can customize colors, add logos, choose frames, adjust sizes, and export in multiple formats (PNG, SVG, PDF).

Do you provide analytics?

Yes, we offer detailed analytics including scan counts, locations, devices, and time-based insights through our dashboard.

Is my data secure?

Your data security is our priority. We use enterprise-grade encryption and follow strict privacy policies to protect your information.

Can I use QRResult for commercial purposes?

Yes, you can use QRResult for both personal and commercial projects without any restrictions or licensing fees.
